About BomaVue

Rooted in Nairobi. Built Around You.

Our Story

Bomavue was born from a simple belief: that buying a home in Nairobi’s most sought-after neighborhoods — Lavington and Westlands — should be a clear, personal, and inspired journey.

As locals who understand the landscape, we saw a gap: too many listings, not enough care. Too much noise, not enough trust. That’s why we created Bomavue — a platform that curates homes with integrity, quality, and a designer’s eye.

We don’t just list properties. We connect people to the right space, in the right place, at the right time. From modern apartments to luxury townhouses, every home we showcase is chosen for its livability, design, and location value. And every buyer we serve is guided with honesty and attention — no jargon, no pressure.

Our Mission

"To connect homebuyers with thoughtfully selected properties in Lavington and Westlands by offering a transparent, design-conscious, and personalized real estate experience rooted in trust and local expertise."
