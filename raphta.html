Raphta Rd.

Westlands

KES.30,000,000
4- Bedroom Maisonette

Located along the serene and tree-lined Raphta Road, this beautifully designed 4-bedroom, 4-bathroom maisonette offers the perfect balance of comfort, space, and modern living. All bedrooms are en-suite, providing privacy and convenience for the whole family. The home features expansive living and dining areas with large windows that let in abundant natural light, a fully fitted kitchen with pantry and laundry space, and high-quality finishes throughout.

Step outside to enjoy a private, landscaped garden ideal for outdoor relaxation or entertaining. The property also includes detached staff quarters, ample parking, and is set within a secure, gated compound with round-the-clock security. With close proximity to top schools, shopping malls, restaurants, hospitals, and major road links, this home is ideal for families or investors seeking an upscale address in the heart of Westlands.
